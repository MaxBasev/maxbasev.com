Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Welcome to maxbasev.com. We value your privacy, and this page outlines how we handle your data (spoiler: we try not to).

How We Use It

Third-Party Services

We may use tools like Google Analytics (very lightly) and payment processors, but they have their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

If you ever want to see what we have on you (or delete it), email i@maxbasev.com. We'll handle it — no drama.
