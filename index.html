About Me

I'm Max — an independent developer building SaaS products, internal dashboards, and automation systems for founders & small teams.

Typical work: internal dashboards, admin panels, product sites, integrations, bots, reporting systems, and custom tools that replace manual workflows.

If your workflow is messy or manual — I can usually turn it into a reliable system.

My core stack is Next.js, Node.js, TypeScript, MongoDB, and Firebase. I ship end-to-end: architecture, implementation, deployment, monitoring, and iteration.

I write at blog.maxbasev.com and publish projects + postmortems at lab.maxbasev.com.

I operate remotely (async-friendly). Sole proprietor (Kyrgyzstan).
